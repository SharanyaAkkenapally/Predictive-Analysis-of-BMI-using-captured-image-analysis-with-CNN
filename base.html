    • Convolution Neural Networks

    Body Mass Index is a measurement of one’s weight with respect to his/her height. It is more an indicator of measurement of one’s total body fat. BMI is very important as it is widely considered in estimating the health conditions. It is said that we have chances of having longer and healthier life with healthy BMI. The main goal is “Can a machine classify the BMI range with the help of just an image?” Being able to identify and classify the BMI range with image analysis can help people control their BMI and maintain a healthier life. This project comes under the domain of Artificial Intelligence (AI). Image analysis makes this very simple to classify the BMI range by analyzing the image using deep learning algorithm named CNN. In this project, the user has to upload the image of a person for image processing. The deep learning model classifies it into any of the three categories: under weight, normal weight, over weight. The input image should be a coloured image and .jpg, .jpeg, .png file. The uploaded image is classified and the output will be anyone of the three above mentioned categories. This project creates a realistic model and it is user friendly so that anyone can easily track their Body Mass Index to have a fit and healthier life.
